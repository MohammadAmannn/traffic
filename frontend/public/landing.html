✦ Process

How the system works

A seamless 4-step pipeline from raw video footage to optimized smart signal timing.

01
Upload / Drag & Drop
Drag & drop 4 traffic video feeds — one per intersection direction with live thumbnail previews generated instantly.
02
YOLOv4 Detection
Each video is analyzed by YOLOv4-Tiny to count and classify vehicles using OpenCV real-time inference per lane.
03
Genetic Optimization
Vehicle counts feed into a genetic algorithm (400 population, 25 iterations) evolving optimal timings using Webster's delay fitness.
04
Results & Export
View animated bar charts, download PDF reports, and save sessions to history for comparison and analytics.
